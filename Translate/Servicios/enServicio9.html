TWO-AXLE DOLLY WITH AIR SUSPENSION

Technical Specifications

Total length:12,600 mm
Total width:2,510 mm
Height at fifth wheel:1,300 mm
Axles:2 axles of 25 tons (each)
Suspension:Pneumatic
Frame:HR A-36 steel
Floor:Alfajor type (non-slip)
Equipment color:Traffic yellow or according to customer requirements
Documentation:Includes registration and measurements according to plan

Advantages and Specifications

The FULL SERVICES two-axle dolly is designed to distribute and transport heavy loads safely and stably, thanks to its robust HR A-36 steel frame. The addition of air suspension provides excellent shock absorption, protecting both the load and the equipment on uneven terrain.

  • Outstanding Capacity: Equipped with two 25-ton axles, providing high load capacity and weight distribution.
  • Air Suspension for a smooth ride, greater stability, and better adaptation to various road conditions.
  • Precise Dimensions: Manufactured to exact specifications, ensuring compliance with exact technical specifications.
  • Non-slip floor: Surface with checkered floor for operator safety and to prevent cargo from sliding.
  • Legal warranty: The equipment is delivered duly registered, ready to operate under current regulations.
  • Optimized total length of 12,600 mm and width of 2,510 mm.
