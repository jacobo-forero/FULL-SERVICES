Advantages and Configurations

The FULL SERVICES Low Bed Semi-Trailer adapts to various heavy and oversized transport needs, offering robust capacities. 25 to 50 tons. Its modular design allows for multiple neck and access configurations, ensuring versatility for the mobilization of machinery and elements of great height or weight.

  • Flexible configuration: Can be configured as a low bed with removable hydraulic neck or fixed neck with access ramp.
  • Ideal for transporting special machinery or oversized items.
  • Includes flags to extend the width of the equipment up to 3 meters.
  • U-type castings for securing and lashing loads with maximum efficiency.
  • Hydraulic cylinder for easy handling.
  • Toolbox on the front for easy access and storage.
  • Built with main beams in A-36 steel or the resistant A-572.
  • Access ramp (for fixed neck) facilitating loading and unloading.
