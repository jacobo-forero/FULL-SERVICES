Our History

In the land of Orito, Putumayo, where oil lay hidden and the ambitions of many gave rise to violence everywhere, a man named Saúl Delgado was born. After overcoming hundreds of difficulties, gaining experience, and determined not to fear the terror induced by criminal organizations operating outside the law, he boldly decided to undertake what is now known as one of the most recognized and leading companies in the provision of oil services in Colombia.

It was on May 24, 1995, that Full Services was officially founded when it obtained a transportation contract with Ecopetrol, making its fleet of trucks and tractor-trailers available, which grew as its revenues and business vision increased. In 1997, Don Saul's eldest son, civil engineer Victor Delgado, joined the company. Together, combining their experience and advanced knowledge and forming a talented team, they managed to begin a phase of diversification that became much more consolidated in 2003 when they acquired a Cooper 550 Work Over machine, which was in poor condition and required a three-year reconstruction effort. Once fully operational, they leased it to the Brisa company, providing Work Over and drilling services within Colombia. This is when Full Services opened its offices in Bogotá, managed by Don Saul Delgado's wife and son, Mrs. Sandra Cisneros and Engineer Victor Delgado.

Today, Full Services has grown to operate in different parts of Colombia, expanding its borders and international relations, with talented teams and an ideal infrastructure to offer a broad portfolio that includes everything related to oil and industrial services, civil works, and certain architectural projects.
Today we continue to grow, driven by our team's commitment and our vision to lead the sector with quality and responsibility.

Corporate Values

INTEGRITY

A fundamental pillar of our existence is that every task we perform is carried out with complete transparency, that everyone who is part of Full Services acts with integrity, always maintaining their reputation and human qualities intact.

WELL-BEING

At Full Services, we are convinced that we are all part of the same family, that we care for each other, collaborate, and respect each other unanimously to achieve a high level of common well-being, avoiding discord or selfishness.

EXCELLENCE

We are a company committed to continuous improvement, attentive to every detail in order to achieve corporate excellence. We know that excellence is achieved every day through results and solutions, not excuses or justifications.

COMMITMENT

When we start any project or operation, we have no excuse for not completing it fully. We have a sense of ownership and responsibility, allowing all our clients to feel supported and confident that we will achieve all the objectives to which we have committed ourselves.
